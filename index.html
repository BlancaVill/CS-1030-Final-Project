What is RoamPack?

RoamPack is designed to help groups plan trips together. It allows users to create itineraries, share travel plans, and collaborate on trip details.

How does it work?

RoamPack allows users to create a group, invite friends, and start planning their next adventure. The platform provides tools for managing schedules, budgets, and activities.
Users can engage in a chat, providing easy communication with all group members. All your trip needs under one place, making group planning a breeze!
